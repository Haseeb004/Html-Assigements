Book Review

Title

The Great Gatsby

Author

F. Scott Fitzgerald

Summary

The Great Gatsby is a classic American novel set in the Jazz Age. It explores themes of wealth, love, and the American Dream through the story of Jay Gatsby, a mysterious millionaire, and his unrequited love for Daisy Buchanan. The book offers a critical look at the decadence and excess of the era, while also delivering a poignant tale of ambition and heartbreak.

Rating

★★★★★
